Welcome

This website is a collaborative project for LIS 500: Code and Power at UW-Madison. Through curated resources, personal reflections, and spotlights on leaders in tech, we examine how technology intersects with race, identity, and power. Our goal is to think critically about who builds technology, who it serves, and whose voices are left out of the conversation.
